Title
Monkey Time

Elavator Pitch
A monkey in a hawaiian shirt catching bananas, by the beach. 

30 second  gameplay description
The gameplay involes a monkey being able to move from side to side to catch falling banans from the sky into his basket

Pillars
Highscore: every five bananas cought in a row gives a bonus.
Customization: Wide variety of shirts to unlock and equip.
Music: There are different tunes that the player can unlock and select to play in the background

Features
In our game after you catch 5 banans in a row the monkey will do a little dance of excitement and also each time you catch a banana you will get a piece of gold that then you can spend in the shop to buy new skins for the monkey and also skins for the bananas
